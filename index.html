Kai Mori is a debut feature film director working between Tokyo and Los Angeles — trained at AFI, shaping stories through stillness, movement, and visual rhythm.

From narrative shorts on 35mm to branded films and experimental installations, the work sits at the intersection of cinema, fashion, and the moving image.

Approach

From script
to screen

A disciplined process rooted in pre-visualization, collaboration on set, and meticulous post — every frame intentional.

01

Develop

Treatment, mood boards, and spatial pre-vis. Story and world-building before a single frame is shot.

02

Shoot

On set with cinematography partners. Blocking, lensing, and performance shaped in real time.

03

Shape

Edit, grade, and sound design. Rhythm and silence treated as equal partners in the cut.

04

Release

Festival strategy, platform delivery, and installation installs for gallery and brand partners.
